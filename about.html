our story

fucculent greetings began with a simple idea: to bring warmth, resilience, and a touch of nature into everyday connections.

inspired by succulents and blooms that thrive in unexpected places, our cards are designed to celebrate life’s moments with charm and authenticity.

whether it’s a birthday, a thank you, or just a hello, we believe in greetings that feel cozy, modern, and full of heart.
